Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Short version: DailyCore does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device. We don't have servers. We don't have accounts (unless you optionally enable Firebase sync). We don't show ads.

1. Who We Are

DailyCore is a personal journaling and habit tracking application. It is operated as an independent project. For questions, contact: hello@dailycore.app

2. Data We Collect

By default: Nothing.

DailyCore stores all data exclusively on your device using the browser's localStorage API. This includes:

  • Your daily journal entries (mood, reflections, scores)
  • Habit completion history
  • Streak counts and XP points
  • App settings and preferences
  • PIN lock (stored as a hashed value, not plain text)

None of this data leaves your device unless you explicitly use the cloud sync feature.

3. Optional Cloud Sync (Firebase)

If you choose to enable cloud sync (requires configuring Firebase), the following applies:

  • Your journal data is stored in your own Firebase Firestore database
  • Authentication is via Google Sign-In (handled by Firebase Auth)
  • We (the app developers) do not have access to your Firebase data
  • Your data is governed by Google Firebase's Privacy Policy
  • You can delete your data at any time from Firebase Console

Cloud sync is disabled by default and requires manual configuration by the user.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ No analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No usage statistics sent to any server
  • ❌ No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • ❌ No crash reporting

5. Permissions Used

Permission Why
Notifications Optional daily reminders to write your entry. Processed locally — not sent to any server.
Microphone Optional voice entry feature. Audio is processed by the device's built-in speech recognition (Web Speech API). Audio is never stored or transmitted.
Internet Loading fonts and chart libraries from CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com, fonts.googleapis.com). No user data is sent.

6. Children's Privacy

DailyCore does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no advertising and no user-to-user communication. It is suitable for all ages.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security is primarily managed by your device's own security (screen lock, encryption). The PIN lock feature adds an additional layer of protection within the app. Your PIN is stored as a hashed value, not as plain text.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:

  • In-app: Settings → Danger Zone → "Saara Data Delete Karo"
  • Android: Settings → Apps → DailyCore → Clear Data
  • Browser: Clear site data for the app's URL

9. Export & Portability

You own your data. You can export a full JSON backup at any time from Settings → Data & Share → JSON Backup Export.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
